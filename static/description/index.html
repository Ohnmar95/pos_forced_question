POS Forced Questions

Keep your customer’s choice on top notch!!

This module is used to ask questions to the customer before adding an item to the POS Cart, questions are like- Small or Large, Vegetarian or Non-Vegetarian and Sweet or Salted etc. and accordingly the selected answer is printed on the receipt. The POS user can even modify the answers even after adding the product to the cart.

What if we know the customer’s choice related to products?

Yes!! It’s possible....POS Forced Questions Module enables the POS users to put up questions. The module allows POS users to ask multiple forced questions about the choice of customers related to products.

Why asking questions to customers at POS stores?

A satisfied customer is the key to increased sales and profits leading to success and enhanced business growth. It not only increases sales but also makes the customer's first choice that makes you stand out of your competitors.

  • Keeping customer’s choices as top priority will help you to attract more potential users to your POS store.
  • It also enables you to know the popular demand of customers that enables you to fetch profitable leads. Also, it is a great way to make yourself stand out of the competitors.
  • So whether the customer likes extra cheese or no cheese, veg or non-veg, double or single toppings, all their preferences will be fulfilled before adding the product in POS.

Detailed Features List


Ask questions to your customers with ease in POS

  • POS Forced Questions enables POS users to ask multiple forced questions about the choice of customers related to products.
  • With this module, you can put up questions to customers to know their product related preferences and serve them better.


Manage the questions list from the Odoo backend

  • You can easily manage and create questions asked on the POS store from the Odoo backend.
  • Seller has the authority to change the question from the backend.


Modify the customers answers in POS

  • The POS user can even modify the answers even after adding the product to the cart.
  • They can add or delete the answer accordingly at the POS store.


View the answers on receipt too

  • The answer will be printed on the receipt (POS BOX and POS TICKET)
  • The selected answer for the product will also be saved in the backend.
  • Pos Session View
  • Backend View
Questions asked on adding product to cart

On adding product to the cart, a question popup will be shown if any question group is selected in product configuration.

Modify answer after adding product

Seller can modifiy answer for the product at any time by clicking 'info icon' shown in orderline.
